GENE QUEST

Welcome to an adventurous journey focused on understanding genes and their functions in genetics
The goal of the game is to be the first player to correctly construct a DNA strand, transcribe it
into an RNA sequence, and then translate it into the correct amino acid chain that matches your hidden
Protein Blueprint Card. The game teaches the central dogma of molecular biology: DNA → RNA → Protein.

Game cards

Guanine

A nucleotide that pairs with Cytosine,
for the stability of the DNA structure.

"Stability Boost" - Increases the defense of your
DNA structure by 2 points for one turn.

Cytosine

A nucleotide that pairs with Guanine,
important for the integrity of genetic information.

"Information Transfer" - Allows you to
swap one card from your hand with a card from the discard pile.

Thymine

A nucleotide that pairs with Adenine, crucial for the formation of DNA strands.

"Replication Shield" - Negates the next attack against your DNA structure

Adenine

A vital nucleotide that pairs with Thymine in DNA

"Pairing Power" - When played, you can draw an extra card if paired with Thymine.

DNA Detective

A card that shows the start of the game

The inspiration behind the game

This card game is inspired by the fascinating world of genetics,
specifically the roles of DNA and RNA in living organisms.
The game aims to educate players about the fundamental building
blocks of life while providing an engaging and strategic gameplay experience.
By incorporating real scientific concepts into the mechanics, players not only
enjoy the thrill of competition but also gain insights into the complexities of genetic
coding and molecular biology.
