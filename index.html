Hand-fan toy

The hand fan is a simple, eco-friendly craft project that reporposes waste materials to create a funcional cooling device. We used a small plastic bottle, a string, cardboard pieces, and cellotape. This device might appear simple, yet it successfully illustrates essential mechanical concepts, creativity, and problem-solving skills. Its purpose is to provide a cooling and playful interaction by producing airflow when rotated using a string.

Biology Card Game

Welcome to the world of GENE QUEST, an exciting card game that immerses players in the intricate realm of genetics! In this game, players take on the roles of geneticists, using DNA and RNA cards to build powerful sequences and outsmart their opponents. With each turn, you'll make tactical decisions, harnessing the unique abilities of nucleotides to create winning combinations. Whether you're a science enthusiast or a casual gamer, GENE QUEST offers a fun and educational experience that challenges your strategic thinking while deepening your understanding of the building blocks of life.
