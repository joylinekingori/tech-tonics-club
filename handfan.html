Our Inspiration

Witnessing the issue of plastic waste, particularly discarded Coca-Cola bottles, sparked our interest to find a resourceful way to give them a new life.

Our journey to create this unique hand fan began with a completely different vision: a miniature water aquarium...

It was then that the idea of our hand fan took flight quite literally we found inspiration in the simple yet intriguing mechanics of it

Our hand fan is more than just a source of cool air; it's a testament to a sustainable design. By utilizing manual power, we've created an eco-friendly alternative

How It Works

These are the materials needed: Waste bottle, scissors, string, glue, bottle caps, stick, carton

Using a pair of scissors cut the carton and bind it into X shape

Now you have your carton fan. Keep going!

Bore a hole into both of the bottle caps

Tie the string onto the stick and assemble everything into this. Ther you go your simple fun hand toy!
